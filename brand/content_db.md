How does it work?
After placing an order, you send us a photo that will be animated when you point your phone camera at it, and a video that will play over your photo. We process your files and send you a QR code for scanning and a link for downloading, if you can’t scan the code.
Then you just scan the code, wait a few seconds for loading, give access to the camera, point the camera at your photo and enjoy the result
