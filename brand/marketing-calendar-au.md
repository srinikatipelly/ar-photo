# The Golden Frame — Australian Marketing Calendar

**Product:** AR "living photos" — bring memories to life. *Where Memories Come Alive.*
**Market:** Australia (AU-specific dates — do NOT copy US timing, especially Father's Day & Thanksgiving).
**Brand:** Gold + green premium luxury feel.

> **How to use this calendar:** Each campaign has a **launch date** (when ads/posts go live) and a **hard cut-off** (last order date that still allows production + delivery before the occasion). Work backwards from the occasion using the lead-time rule below.

## Lead-time rule (production + delivery buffer)
Because these are personalised keepsakes, customers must order early. Suggested buffers:
- **Digital-only delivery (QR + link):** cut-off **3–4 days** before the occasion.
- **Physical framed product (posted):** cut-off **10–14 days** before (allow AU Post transit + peak-season delays).
- **Christmas:** treat cut-offs as **firm** and communicate them loudly from mid-November.

## Strategic notes
- **Three anchor campaigns** to build the year around: **Father's Day (Sep) → Christmas (Dec) → Mother's Day (May)**, plus **Valentine's (Feb)**.
- **21st birthdays** deserve a dedicated always-on campaign — culturally bigger in AU than the US.
- **Memorial keepsakes** are the emotional differentiator: low competition, perfect AR use case, pairs with ANZAC Day.
- **Region-lock your ads:** never run US Father's Day (June) or Thanksgiving creative to AU audiences.
- **B2B / small-business angle** (AR on products, greeting cards, packaging) fits EOFY (June) and Christmas retail ramp.
